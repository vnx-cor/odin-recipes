Lemon Garlic Asparagus

Seraphina “Sera” Soufflé | April 30, 2026

Bright and buttery, this Lemon Garlic Asparagus showcases tender asparagus spears finished with a vibrant lemon-garlic butter sauce that elevates simple spring vegetables into an elegant side dish. Whether steamed, roasted, grilled, or blanched, the spears are kissed with aromatic garlic, fresh lemon zest, and parsley — making this quick and easy recipe perfect for weeknights or special occasions alike.

Ingredients Needed

  • 1 pound asparagus, washed and trimmed
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 large garlic cloves, minced
  • ½ large lemon (juice and zest), plus more for garnish (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh parsley
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper (to taste)

Time to Get Cooking!

  1. Prepare the asparagus
    • Wash the asparagus and trim off the tough woody ends.
    • Pat dry to help with even cooking.
  2. Heat the pan
    • Heat a large skillet over medium heat.
    • Add the butter and olive oil, letting the butter melt completely and foam slightly.
  3. Cook the garlic
    • Add the minced garlic to the pan.
    • Sauté for 30–45 seconds, stirring constantly, until fragrant but not browned.
  4. Sauté the asparagus
    • Add the asparagus to the skillet in a single layer.
    • Season with kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper.
    • Cook for 5–7 minutes, turning occasionally, until the asparagus is tender-crisp and lightly browned.
  5. Add lemon
    • Reduce heat to low.
    • Stir in the lemon juice and lemon zest, tossing to coat the asparagus evenly.
    • Cook for 1 more minute to warm through and brighten the flavors.
  6. Finish and garnish
    • Remove from heat and sprinkle with minced parsley.
    • Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
    • Garnish with extra lemon zest or lemon wedges if desired.
  7. Serve
    • Serve immediately as a fresh side dish alongside chicken, fish, or pasta.
