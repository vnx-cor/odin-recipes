Creamy Spinach Pasta

Gideon “Grit” Pâté | September 27, 2025

This Creamy Spinach Pasta is a simple yet indulgent Italian-American pasta dish featuring sautéed garlic in browned butter, a rich half-and-half cream sauce seasoned with herbs and a bright hint of lemon, and plenty of tender spinach folded in with Parmesan-kissed noodles. It delivers serious restaurant quality in about 30 minutes — perfect for a comforting weeknight meal.

What to Have Ready

  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon dried parsley
  • ½ teaspoon mustard powder
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • 4 tablespoons salted butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup half-and-half (half milk/half cream)
  • 3 teaspoons lemon juice
  • ¾ cup Parmesan cheese, finely grated from a block
  • 1½ cups spinach (fresh)
  • ½ pound ziti rigati (penne also works)

Let’s Make It!

  1. Cook the pasta
    • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
    • Add ½ pound ziti rigati and cook until al dente, about 8–10 minutes (follow package directions).
    • Reserve ½ cup of pasta water, then drain the pasta and set aside.
  2. Make the creamy sauce base
    • In a large skillet or saucepan, melt 4 tablespoons butter over medium heat.
    • Add the minced garlic and sauté for 30 seconds, until fragrant (don’t let it brown).
  3. Thicken the sauce
    • Sprinkle in 2 tablespoons flour and stir constantly for 1–2 minutes until it forms a smooth paste (roux).
  4. Add liquids
    • Slowly pour in 1 cup chicken broth, whisking constantly to prevent lumps.
    • Add 1 cup half-and-half, continuing to whisk until smooth.
  5. Season the sauce
    • Stir in:
      • ½ tsp dried oregano
      • ½ tsp dried parsley
      • ½ tsp mustard powder
      • ¼ tsp garlic powder
      • 3 tsp lemon juice
    • Simmer the sauce for 3–5 minutes, until it begins to thicken.
  6. Add cheese & spinach
    • Lower heat to low and add ¾ cup Parmesan, stirring until melted and creamy.
    • Add 1½ cups fresh spinach and cook 1–2 minutes, until wilted.
  7. Combine pasta & sauce
    • Add the cooked pasta to the skillet.
    • Toss well to coat, adding reserved pasta water a little at a time if needed to loosen the sauce.
  8. Serve
    • Plate the pasta and garnish with extra Parmesan if desired.
    • Enjoy warm!
