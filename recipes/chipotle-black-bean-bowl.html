Chipotle Black Bean Bowl

Violet Crust | June 2, 2025

A Chipotle Black Bean Bowl is a vibrant, nutrient-packed meal that brings together smoky, chipotle-seasoned black beans with bright, fresh vegetables and hearty grains for a satisfying, Southwestern-inspired bowl. It balances spicy chipotle heat with citrus brightness and earthy beans to deliver bold flavor and fiber-rich fuel — perfect as a wholesome lunch or dinner and versatile enough for meal-prep bowls.

What to Gather

  • Cooked rice (white, brown, or cilantro-lime) or another grain of choice
  • Black beans (canned or cooked)
  • Chipotle peppers in adobo sauce (minced) — adds smoky heat
  • Olive oil (for sautéing)
  • Aromatics: diced red onion, garlic
  • Bell pepper (optional)
  • Smoked paprika and other spices (e.g., coriander, oregano)
  • Corn (seared or grilled)
  • Cherry tomatoes or diced fresh tomato
  • Cilantro
  • Avocado or guacamole
  • Lime wedges for finishing (optional)
  • Kale or other greens
  • Chipotle-flavored sauce, mayo, or crema
  • Red onion or diced raw onion

Time to Cook!

  1. Prep your base
    • Cook rice or grain according to package instructions (or use leftover cooked rice).
    • Warm the rice and set aside.
  2. Sauté the aromatics
    • Heat 1–2 tablespoons olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
    • Add diced red onion and sauté for 2–3 minutes, until softened.
    • Add minced garlic and cook for another 30 seconds.
  3. Add the veggies
    • If using, add diced bell pepper and cook 3–4 minutes, until tender.
    • Add corn (seared or grilled) and cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Make the chipotle black beans
    • Add black beans to the skillet.
    • Stir in minced chipotle peppers in adobo (start with 1 pepper and adjust to taste).
    • Season with:
      • Smoked paprika
      • Coriander
      • Oregano
      • Salt & pepper
    • Cook 5–7 minutes, until heated through and flavors combine.
    • Optional: mash a few beans slightly for a creamier texture.
  5. Prepare the toppings
    • While the beans cook, prep:
      • Cherry tomatoes (halved) or diced tomatoes
      • Chopped cilantro
      • Avocado slices or guacamole
      • Kale or greens (toss with a little lime juice and salt if desired)
      • Raw red onion (for crunch)
  6. Build the bowls
    • In each bowl, add a layer of rice or grain.
    • Add a handful of kale or greens.
    • Top with chipotle black beans & veggies.
    • Add corn, tomatoes, cilantro, and avocado.
    • Drizzle with chipotle sauce, mayo, or crema.
  7. Finish & serve
    • Add a squeeze of lime juice over the top.
    • Serve warm and enjoy!
