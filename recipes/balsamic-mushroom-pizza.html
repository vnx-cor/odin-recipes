Balsamic Mushroom Pizza

Nico Sizzle | March 14, 2025

A refined twist on classic mushroom pizza, this Balsamic Mushroom Pizza layers sautéed mushrooms with a hint of balsamic vinegar that adds rich sweetness and tangy depth to every bite. Often paired with complementary cheeses (like goat cheese, fontina, or provolone) and optional greens such as spinach, the balsamic accent elevates earthy mushrooms into a flavorful, balanced topping that’s both gourmet and comforting.

What You’ll Need

  • Pizza dough (homemade or store-bought) – flour, yeast, salt, water, olive oil
  • Mixed mushrooms (e.g., cremini, button, shiitake), sliced
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar (used to sauté mushrooms or as a drizzle)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Shallots or onions (optional)
  • Salt & black pepper to taste
  • Mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • Goat cheese (crumbled) — adds a creamy tang
  • Spinach or other greens (optional)
  • Other cheeses (fontina, provolone, ricotta) depending on variation
  • Additional balsamic glaze or vinegar drizzle after baking
  • Fresh herbs (optional: thyme, basil)

Let's Cook!

  1. Preheat & prep
    • Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
    • If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven while it heats.
    • If using store-bought dough, let it come to room temperature for 15–20 minutes.
  2. Sauté the mushrooms
    • Heat 1–2 tablespoons olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
    • Add sliced mushrooms and cook for 5–7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    • Add minced garlic and shallots/onions (if using) and cook for 1–2 minutes.
    • Drizzle in balsamic vinegar (about 1–2 tablespoons) and cook for another 2–3 minutes, until mushrooms are glossy and slightly caramelized.
    • Season with salt & black pepper to taste.
    • Remove from heat and set aside.
  3. Stretch the dough
    • On a lightly floured surface, stretch or roll out the dough into your desired shape (round or rectangle).
    • Transfer to a pizza peel or baking sheet dusted with cornmeal.
  4. Assemble the pizza
    • Lightly brush the dough with olive oil (optional).
    • Sprinkle shredded mozzarella evenly over the dough.
    • Add the sautéed balsamic mushrooms on top.
    • Add crumbled goat cheese and any optional cheeses (fontina, provolone, ricotta).
    • Add spinach or greens if using.
  5. Bake
    • Bake in the preheated oven for 10–12 minutes, until the crust is golden and cheese is melted and bubbly.
    • If using a pizza stone, slide the pizza directly onto the stone for best results.
  6. Finish & serve
    • Remove from oven and let cool for 2 minutes.
    • Drizzle with balsamic glaze or a light splash of balsamic vinegar.
    • Sprinkle with fresh herbs (thyme or basil).
    • Slice and enjoy!
